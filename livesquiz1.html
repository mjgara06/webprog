The GIMP 2.2 pour PC, Mac et Linux



Author: Julien Pons
Format: Paperback, 300pp.
ISBN:2746028565
Date: October 2005
Website:http://www.p-n.fr/juilenpons


Description:An in-depth reference for the GIMP than, like the rest of French publisher ENI's Studio Factory series , is aimed at the professional user. This book covers the interface, GIMP functions, and GIMP concepts such as layers and drawables, and also includes a chapter for Photoshop users migrating to the GIMP.
